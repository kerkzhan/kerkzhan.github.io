Carb Count

An educational game created in collaboration from students from IMU to teach young children about carbohydrates and sugar levels.

Carb Count (2017)

A friend who studies at a well known medical university reached out to me to collaborate on her project. She wanted to create an educational game for children to understand the basics of sugar intake. We worked together to discuss the game mechanics as well as the learning outcomes of the game. She presented the game in her final pitch, and was also chosen to bring her project to a conference. As someone who is passionate about education, this was a big step for me at the time to be able to work on a game like this.

Bahadur

Top down dungeon crawler with elemental mechanics.

Bahadur

This game was created as part of my studies and for a subject where we had to make a game from start to finish in 3 months, following industry standards and procedures. I worked in a group of 1 artist, 2 game designers, and 2 other game programmers. I spent majority of my time prototyping a procedural level generator similar to the game "The Binding of Isaac". Unfortunately, the mechanic was imperfect and we had to change to manually designed levels instead. However, my time was well spent as it improved my understanding of random seeding, arrays, nodes and creating algorithms for procedural generation.
